What is ABACC

ABACC spread its Bangladeshi culture and values among local people by providing services targeted to newcomers, youth, women, and seniors. ABACC organizes Bangladeshi significant day events such as International Mother Language Day, Bangladeshi Independence Day and Victory Day to emphasise the importance of these days among our local community and future generations. ABACC create a common platform whereby all community individual is given the opportunity to take an interest and contribute to advancement in community arranging, coordination and execution. ABACC is a place where culture gets merged and creates opportunity for all age people.
